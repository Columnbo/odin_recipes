Mai Tai Recipe

My favorite cocktail, the Mai Tai!

Ingredients

  • 2oz Jamaican Rum
  • 1/2oz Orange Curacao
  • 1/2oz Orgeat
  • 1/4oz Simple Syrup
  • Juice from one whole lime

Steps

  1. Half fill a shaker with crushed ice
  2. Add all the ingredients
  3. Shake Vigorously until ice is mainly melted
  4. Pour into Tiki Glass, or small tumbler, quarter filled with ice
  5. Top with half a lime, and a sprig of mint
